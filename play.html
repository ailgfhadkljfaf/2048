How to Play 2048

The objective of the game is to slide numbered tiles on a grid to combine them and create a tile with the number 2048.

Here are the basic rules:

  • Use the arrow keys on your keyboard to move the tiles up, down, left, or right.
  • When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one tile with their combined value.
  • Each move adds a new tile (with a value of 2 or 4) to a random empty spot on the board.
  • The game ends when there are no more valid moves left (i.e., no empty spaces and no adjacent tiles with the same number).

Try to reach the 2048 tile, but feel free to keep playing to achieve higher scores!
