How to Play 2048 (Detailed explanation)

1.Game Setup

  • The game is played on a 4x4 grid.
  • At the start of the game, two tiles with the number 2 or 4 will appear in random empty spots on the grid.

2.Controls

  • PC/Keyboard: Use the arrow keys to move the tiles up, down, left, or right (↑ ↓ ← →).
  • Mobile/Touch: Swipe your finger on the screen to move the tiles up, down, left, or right.
  • Every move shifts all tiles in that direction.

3.Merging Rules

  • When two tiles of the same number collide, they merge into one tile with the sum of their values.
    • For example, two tiles with the number 4 will merge to form a single tile with the number 8 (4 + 4 = 8).
  • After every move, a new tile (2 or 4) appears in a random empty spot on the grid.

4.Objective

  • The goal is to create a tile with the number 2048 by merging tiles.
  • You can continue playing even after reaching the 2048 tile to achieve higher scores.

5.Losing Conditions

  • The game ends when there are no more valid moves left, meaning:
    • There are no empty spaces on the grid.
    • No adjacent tiles have the same number and cannot be merged.
